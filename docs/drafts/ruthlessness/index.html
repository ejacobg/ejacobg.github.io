Ruthlessness

I notice that I sometimes read parts of a book that I don’t have a particular need for at the moment. I feel that I can skip many parts of the books that I read, especially if I know I won’t be using that information for a while.

At the same time, I do feel some pull toward reading those sections, but I’m not sure if this is because of any avoidant behavior (eg. procrastinating from what I’m really trying to do, which is get a job ASAP).

I guess the balance is to just skim the sections I feel aren’t as important, and write down only the most poignant parts.
