Weeds and Clouds

It’s kinda frustrating putting in the work to chase my goals, but not really feeling much progress toward them. I know I’m still very early in my career, but that’s part of the issue. It feels like I’ve put in all these hours just to get to the starting line. How much longer will I have to keep at this to become half the programmer I imagine myself to be? I can dream and follow roadmaps all I like, but the actual path I tread is so much different than the route I planned for myself. Today I spent 47 minutes trying to get a stupid fucking datalist element to render correctly, and barely accomplished even that. How did this exercise make me better than yesterday? It’s hard to keep aligned with my long-term vision while being tasked with items that are only tangentially related to them. It’s hard to see progress when I’m lost in the weeds. At the same time though, dreaming and planning with my head in the clouds won’t get me any closer to those goals either.

I know that I just so happen to be stuck with some frontend work for the time being. It’s part of what I signed up for, so I can’t complain too much. Like with anything else, I think I need to get used to it. I was thrown into this project without a lot of experience using the framework (or frontend work in general), and that’s where a lot of my frustration comes from. I need a better grasp of how well I’m meeting my goals, as well as a better mindset about the work that I’m currently doing. I can afford to take a couple of detours, and in fact the Year of Diversification demands it.
