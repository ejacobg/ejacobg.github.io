Work Hours

Either I’m lazy or people have a loose definition of “work”. 6 hours/day or 50/week seems very insane. Do some research/write a blog post about this (“people don’t work as much as they say” article).

I often wonder whether I’m actually doing “enough” to get myself employed or whether I’m just a lazy fuck. A lot of the “job searching hell” articles or threads may or may not exaggerate the amount of work actually being put into this. It just seems crazy for these job-seekers to be prescribing 8+ hour workdays and 100+ sent applications just for an entry-level job. I understand that the junior level is very oversaturated, but these numbers are almost comical if it weren’t for how common they are. Even on my best of best days, I can track maybe 6 hours of real butt-in-seat work. Maybe.

I’m either an impostor or not doing enough.
