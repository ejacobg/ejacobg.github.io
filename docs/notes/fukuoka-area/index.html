Dazaifu

Dazaifu is accessible via Nishitetsu’s Tenjin Omuta Line. Starting from Nishitetsu Fukuoka (Tenjin), take an Express or Limited Express service to Nishitetsu Futsukaichi, then transfer to the Nishitetsu Dazaifu Line for Dazaifu.

Service Destination Fare Travel Time
Local Futsukaichi 370¥ 30min
Express Futsukaichi 370¥ 15min
Limited Express Futsukaichi 370¥ 15min
Local Futsukaichi->Dazaifu 170¥ 7min

Everything in the Dazaifu itinerary is walkable.

Kitakyushu (北九州)

From Hakata, Kitakyushu is reachable via the Sanyo Shinkansen or the Kagoshima Main Line. The major station is Kokura.

Mojiko (門司港)

From Kokura, take the Kagoshima Main Line to its terminus at Mojiko.

Mt. Sakurakura (皿倉山)

Google Maps

https://www.sarakurayama-cablecar.co.jp/shuttlebus/

There exists a dedicated shuttle bus to the cable car from Yahata Station.

42 Kurosaki-Hobashira-Edamitsu-Higashi Otani-Tobata [Nishitetsu Bus]

From Yahata Station, take the Nishitetsu Bus 42 towards Tobihata Gate (製鉄飛幡門). Get off at Hobashira Tozanguchi (帆柱登山口). The Sakurayama Cablecar is walkable from here. 280 yen, 13 minutes.

  • This route is not found by Google Maps; it tends to start from Edamitsu Station, taking line 42 in the opposite direction (towards Kurosaki (黒崎) Bus Center/Yahata Station).

Nishitetsu does have a route finder if you need more info.
